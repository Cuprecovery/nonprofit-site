Our Stories & Testimonies

At CUP-RECOVERY, our foundation is built on real lives transformed by the power of God. Many of us have personally walked through addiction, brokenness, incarceration, and hopelessness. Through Jesus Christ, we have experienced freedom, restoration, and new purpose.

These are not secondhand stories. They are lived testimonies of what God can and will do. We believe recovery is not just sobriety, but complete heart and life transformation through faith in Christ.

Training & Calling

In obedience to God's calling, we are actively pursuing education and certification in peer support and case management. Our goal is to combine lived experience, professional training, and biblical truth to better serve men seeking lasting recovery.

Isaiah 40:31 - Those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength.

Ezekial 36:26 - I will give you a new heart and put a new spirit within you.

2 Corinthians 3:17 - Where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is freedom.

By equipping ourselves with both faith-based principles and evidence-based support skills, we are preparing to walk alongside others with compassion, accountability, and practical guidance. Why We Exist

We exist to help men break free from addiction and life-controlling struggles through the transforming power of Jesus Christ.

  • To help men break free from addiction and destructive cycles
  • To provide Christ-centered recovery and peer support
  • To restore purpose, identity, and faith through discipleship
  • To train men in peer support and case management rooted in biblical truth
